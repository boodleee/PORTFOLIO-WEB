What is the website all about?

Flow

Flow is not just a portfolio website—it’s a platform that creatively combines professional work with a powerful message. Designed with the mission of promoting SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation, Flow aims to raise awareness about the importance of clean water for all, while showcasing the work of the designer behind the site. The website features a sleek, minimalist design that symbolizes purity and clarity, much like the water it advocates for. With an easy-to-navigate layout and responsive design, Flow makes it simple for visitors to explore the owner’s projects while learning about SDG 6 and its importance. The site’s structure mirrors the idea of smooth, uninterrupted flow, making it user-friendly while promoting a cause that impacts billions globally.
